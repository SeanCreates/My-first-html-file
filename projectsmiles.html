A smile a day...

Keeps the blues away.

Smiling is something I wish I practiced sooner.No matter the weather,or the season,I've come to learn that its good for me and it makes me feel better .It is something I am still working on and I hope to perfect on it and be the best at feeling exactly how i am supposed to feel .when i am supposed to be feeling it.

DID YOU KNOW THAT IT TAKES MORE MUSCLES ON YOUR FACE TO FROWN THAN IT TAKES TO SMILE. This means it is more tideous to wear a frown when you can choose to see the silver lining to a dark cloud otherwise seeing the glass as half filled in stead of half empty.

However,as much as a smile is basically my motto in life,it has been argued time and again that 'toxic positivity' exists and that sometimes it is better to call a spade a spade. I strongly agree with this and believe that we should feel all that we need to feel then move on. Hogging and bottling up feelings is very much toxic and i definitely advocate that we accept and feel those feelings and once we move on, smile like never before.Like the sun emerging after a dark storm.

">In conclusion and in the wise words of Sia, 'YOU' RE NEVER FULLY DRESSED WITHOUT A SMILE.'
